Vision
All organizations (public or private) have one thing in common- they are made up of people. However, majority of organizations present an image that hides most of their people strctures making them feel inaccessible and opaque.

Orgpedia aims enable organizations to shine light on their people and their structures.

Mission
  • Orgpedia will collate publicly available information about organizations and present the information about people and their roles, responsibilities, hierarchies, budgets, jurisdictions, authority.
  • Given the public nature of the information it will provide an easy way to share, study and document organizations.
  • Orgpedia will allow people to build organization information either in public or free domain.
